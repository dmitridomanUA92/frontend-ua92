About HTMLemon

HTMLemon is a tiny tech blog that squeezes sour bugs into lemonade. Short fixes, plain English, and a bit of fun.

We explain why pages break (wonky HTML tags, missing CSS files, scripts loading too late) and how to un‑break them in a few simple steps.

No jargon marathons. Just small examples, clear headings, and basic accessibility so everyone can follow along.

What you’ll find

  • Quick fixes you can try right now
  • “Why it broke” in one line
  • Tips that won’t wreck your layout
