HTMLemon is a small tech blog exploring common front-end development faults and their fixes. The goal of this blog is to help junior developers identify and resolve common issues they may encounter while working on front-end projects. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn more about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, or an experienced developer looking for tips and tricks, HTMLemon has something for you. Stay tuned for regular updates and new posts!
